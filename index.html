Volleyball

Volleyball is a fun game where two teams, with six players each, play with a ball and a net. Players hit the ball over the net to make it land on the other teamâ€™s side. Each team tries to stop the ball from touching the ground on their side. You can use your hands or arms to pass, set, or hit the ball.

The game starts with a serve, and the ball goes back and forth until it hits the ground or goes out. If your team wins the rally, you get a point. The team with the most points wins the game.
